ALL SCI-FI

INDY-FI is a sci-fi brand that presents thought-provoking visions of the future. From feature films to short films, series and podcasts, INDY-FI works with established and emerging voices. The current content library consists of more than 400 cutting-edge stories. Recent projects include the SXSW 2018 feature film "Above The Ground", the Youtube Watch series “Anica”, and short film "The Man On The Inside" starring Landon Ayers. Having launched in December 2020, INDY-FI is the brand’s post cable network with current and upcoming distribution on Roku, DirecTV, Comcast, Vudu, Sling, Vizio, Samsung, NBC Watchback, Sinclair and more.
